An artist that is ready to expand on her styles and learn how to create more styles. As someone with a background in art history, I have knowledge about many kinds of styles and the tactics used to create them, and I would like to share my knowledge with others who are interested in many types of artwork. From illuminated drawings to cubist-style paintings, I enjoy recreating many famous styles and giving my works to anyone who wants them. While not made of the exact same materials used centuries ago, I can create artworks of similar appearances.

If owning an artwork sounds like it is too expensive or takes up too much space, I also offer simple tutoring where I go into depth over a variety of topics of historical artworks and methods used to create them. I am someone that believes in spreading knowledge, and my knowledge resides in the art styles that helped build and enculturate the world. It would be my honor to share that knowledge with those who want to know more.

Along with my being commissioned to do works of art and tutoring about the specifics of art history, I also offer art tutoring, too. If anyone wants to learn how to do the art I can or teach about, I am also very willing to teach that as well. Anyone can do art, all it takes is a start and a helping hand, which I am more than happy to be. The best way to leave your mark on the world is through creation. From simple creations such as puppets, to more complicated works with clay and stone, and even digital creations, I can offer help to all sorts of people and any kind of project.
